Education

There is a big number of talented students who have all the skills but cannot afford good education. AL-Shah Foundation identifies such talented poor students and help them by paying their tuition fee, stationary and books, and their fooding and lodging.

Health & Hygiene

Health is also a big issue in our country. Most health hazards are due to poor hygiene in the slums. AL-Shah make all efforts to educate the residents of such areas to be cautious about health & hygiene. Al-Shah also provide medical assistance to the poor and needy patients

About AL-Shah Foundation

Al-Shah Foundation is a beacon of hope that touches the lives of many through a multifaceted approach to social betterment. They are dedicated to addressing key areas such as education, women’s empowerment, eye vision, and health, working tirelessly to uplift communities and individuals.

  • Education: Al-Shah Foundation recognizes the transformative power of education. They invest in educational programs that improve access to quality schooling and provide scholarships to bright minds who need support. By promoting education, they aim to break the cycle of poverty and offer young people the keys to a brighter future.
  • Women Empowerment: The foundation is deeply committed to empowering women. They provide training, resources, and opportunities to help women become self-sufficient and confident contributors to their communities. By supporting women’s empowerment, Al-Shah Foundation is not only fostering gender equality but also nurturing leadership and resilience.
  • Eye Vision: Al-Shah Foundation is dedicated to improving eye vision and eye health in the communities they serve. They organize eye care programs, provide eye exams, and distribute eyeglasses. This focus on eye vision is a testament to their commitment to the overall well-being of individuals.
  • Health: The foundation also recognizes that health is a fundamental component of a thriving community. They actively engage in healthcare programs, organize health camps, and raise awareness about crucial health issues. By promoting health and wellness, Al-Shah Foundation ensures that individuals can lead healthier, more fulfilling lives, free from preventable diseases.
